Chapter 03 · The Ascent

Into Thin Air

Lijiang & Jade Dragon Snow Mtn

After trading in Dali, the path turns steep and perilous as we face the towering Jade Dragon Snow Mountain.

Ancient Times:
This was the "Gate of Hell" for the caravans. As they ascended above 3,000 meters, altitude sickness and sudden snowstorms claimed many lives. Knowledge of the terrain was limited to rumors.

Modern Data Solution:
We map the danger. The map highlights Elevation zones. Red points warn of high-altitude areas (>3000m), allowing you to prepare oxygen and gear in advance.
